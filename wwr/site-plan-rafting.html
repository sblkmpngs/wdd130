Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar has a singular and straight forward purpose, and that is provide the best white water rafting experience you and your family could ever have. We provide an array of activities for every member of your family, from beginner routes for both adults and children to professional routes for experienced rafters. And upon request you can book our exclusive routes, 'THE HONEYMOON ROUTES' for you and your partner to enjoy.

Audience

Dry Oar has professional employees with years of experience ready to teach and guide you on our routes. Everyone is welcome at Dry Oar from the ages of 8 to 75. We at Dry Oar have taken the liberty of having two helicopters which are always ready to lend assistance if needed. The Dry Oar website has a very user friendly interface with easy and straight forward steps and guidlines to help you navigate the site and we also have our contact details within the site with our friendly staff ready to be of assistance to you. The site can be accessed by all internet able devices.
